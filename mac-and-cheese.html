Mac and Cheese

DESCRIPTION:

Mac and Cheese is a typical American pasta dish with lots of cheese sauce.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup uncooked macaroni.
  • 1 cup cheddar.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 cup milk.
  • butter.
  • Grated parmesan cheese

STEPS:

  1. Cook the pasta. Drain it.
  2. Place layers of the pasta and cheese in the baking dish.
  3. In a bowl, beat the egg, milk, salt, until blended.
  4. Pour this mixture over the pasta.
  5. Dot with butter and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes.

Lasagna

DESCRIPTION:

The lasagna is composed by layers of pasta with delicous red sauce of minced meat, covered in cheese, milk cream, onions and oregano.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500g Lasagna noodles
  • 2kg Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Onions and garlic
  • Milk cream

STEPS:

  1. Prepare the ground beef with tomato sauce, onion and garlic.
  2. Assemble the lasagna starting with the ground beef, then a layer of lasagna noodles, and another of ground beef. Repeat this process until the last layer is ground meat.
  3. Add a generous layer of cheese.
  4. Add a generous layer of milk cream.
  5. Add the sliced ​​onions.
  6. Add the oregano.
  7. Bake until golden.

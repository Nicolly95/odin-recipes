Brazilian Hot Dog

DESCRIPTION:

The Brazilian hot dog is very traditional at birthday parties, and in this reinterpretation of the hot dog it is made with chopped sausage with red sauce and straw potatoes on top.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1kg sausage.
  • Tomato sauce.
  • 2 corn can.
  • Onions and garlic.
  • Parsley (optional).
  • A lot of mini breads.

STEPS:

  1. Prepare the base of the sauce by saut√©ing the onion, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  2. Add the chopped sausages and turn off the heat after 1 minute.
  3. Cut the breads in half.
  4. Add generous amounts of sausage sauce to the breads.
  5. Add straw potatoes and parsley on top.
